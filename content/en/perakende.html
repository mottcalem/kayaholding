Kaya Holding, a pioneer in every sector it serves, brought more than 50 years of experience and reliability to the water sector by establishing its Abant, Gölcük, and Burdur facilities.

Abant Water draws its name from Abant, a natural wonder with one of our country’s richest floras, protected as a nature park—the homeland of pure, healthy water.

Produced and offered to modern world standards in hygienic, environmentally friendly processes, Abant Water continues its mission as a pioneer in premium aesthetic glass bottles (0.33 cl and 0.75 cl) that perfectly preserve its natural character, purity, and taste.
