Uninterrupted and Sustainable Power

B.Kaya Elektrik Üretim A.Ş. was established to realize Kaya Holding’s forward-looking vision in energy. The company’s primary goal is to supply sustainable, uninterrupted energy to facilities within Kaya Holding through environmentally friendly approaches.

B.Kaya Elektrik Üretim A.Ş. carries out electricity generation activities in compliance with applicable regulations and is actively involved in establishing and commissioning power generation facilities, energy production and sales, with a focus on wind and solar energy.

Renewable Energy

Solar Power Plants (SPP)

We combine the limitless power of the sun with technology and generate zero-emission energy through Solar Power Plants (SPP) installed on the roofs of our tourism facilities and on large land investments.

We protect nature while minimizing the group’s operational energy costs, securing our steps toward sustainable growth.

A Clean Future

Wind Power Plants (WPP)

As part of our clean energy investments, we contribute to the national grid with modern wind turbines that convert wind power into electricity.

In line with our goal of reducing dependence on fossil fuels, our wind energy projects create regional employment while adding value to our country’s energy independence.

Energy Efficiency

Smart Energy Management and Sustainability

Beyond production, we center efficiency in consumption and use smart energy monitoring systems (Smart Grid) in hotels, factories, and construction projects across the holding.

Aligned with Kaya Holding’s “Zero Carbon” vision, we track every watt of energy, prevent waste, and maximize environmental benefit.
