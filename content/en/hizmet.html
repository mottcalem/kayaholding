Travel & Accommodation

CCK Travel

CCK Travel is a select travel agency within Kaya Holding that handles sales for hotels in the Kaya Hotels & Resorts and Kaya Palazzo Hotels & Resorts chains.

It provides accommodation at Kaya Hotels & Resorts properties, ticket sales, hotel–airport transfers, private aircraft charter, and VIP event solutions.

Private Aviation

Kaya Air & Kaya Hangar

Kaya Air provides premium private aviation services with a Bombardier Challenger 650 aircraft on domestic and international operations.

Located in the Private Hangars Area of the West Apron at Istanbul Atatürk Airport General Aviation Terminal, Kaya Air and Hangar serves distinguished guests with a modern, secure infrastructure equipped with the latest technology on 1,500 m² of built area.

Fuel & Energy

Dorukkaya Petrol

Since 1990, DorukKaya Petrol has operated on the Ankara TEM highway as a trusted fuel station affiliated with Kaya Holding’s Turkish Petroleum chain.

With its location, spacious service area, and corporate standards, it offers all motorists quality fuel alongside superior customer satisfaction.
