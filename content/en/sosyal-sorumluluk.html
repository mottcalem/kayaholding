Corporate Citizenship

For a Sustainable Future

Since our founding, Kaya Holding has embraced the philosophy of sharing what we earn with society. We take a people- and nature-centered approach, not one focused on profit alone.

Through our projects we support equal opportunity in education, carry out afforestation to prevent environmental damage, and undertake nationwide sponsorships to protect our cultural heritage. We work wholeheartedly to leave future generations a more livable Turkey.

Education

Kaya Education Foundation (KEV)
Leaders of Tomorrow Program

KEV reaches high-achieving young people across Turkey with limited financial means and provides thousands of university students with non-repayable scholarships each year.

Alongside the scholarship program, vocational high schools and dormitory buildings we build play an active role in bringing a qualified workforce into the economy. Tomorrow’s leaders are shaped by Kaya Holding’s deep-rooted vision.

Environment and Sustainability

Breath for Tomorrow Forests
Carbon Neutral Goal

In line with our holding’s “Zero Carbon” vision, we establish memorial forests across Anatolia in cooperation with the TEMA Foundation and the General Directorate of Forestry.

More than two million saplings planted to date both help revitalize regional ecosystems and allow us to naturally neutralize our operational carbon footprint.

Culture and Arts

Heritage of Anatolia
Restoration and Arts Support

We believe a society’s strength comes from honoring its past and culture. We support restoration of our country’s ancient heritage as a lead sponsor.

From archaeological excavations to contemporary art exhibitions and symphony orchestras, we support the arts on a broad scale and invest in enriching society’s cultural outlook.
