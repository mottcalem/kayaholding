Corporate Policies

As Kaya Holding, we conduct all our activities with an ethical, sustainable, transparent, and responsible management approach and aim to create long-term value for our stakeholders.

Human Resources Policy Social Responsibility Policy Environmental Responsibility Policy Quality Policy

Human Resources Policy

At Kaya Holding, we believe our employees are our most valuable asset and the foundation of our success. Our top priority is to create an innovative, dynamic corporate culture with a strong sense of belonging and to provide a transparent, fair, and sustainable work environment.

  • Equal Opportunity: In all recruitment, assignment, and promotion processes, we offer merit-based equal opportunities without discrimination based on language, religion, race, or gender.
  • Continuous Development: We support our employees’ professional and personal development through ongoing training programs so they can adapt to the changing global competitive environment.
  • Occupational Health and Safety: In all sectors we operate in, we provide a safe and healthy work environment to international standards, with a zero workplace accident target.
  • Performance and Rewards: To increase productivity, we reward success through measurable and transparent performance evaluation systems.

Social Responsibility Policy

Kaya Holding is an organization that complements its business success with a sense of responsibility toward society. With our corporate citizenship approach, we develop sustainable projects across education, the environment, culture and the arts, and social solidarity, and our primary goal is to contribute long term to improving social welfare.

  • Children’s Rights: At Kaya Holding, we regard children’s right to grow up in safe, healthy, and supportive environments as a fundamental responsibility. In all our activities we adopt an approach respectful of children’s rights and act with zero tolerance toward child labor, neglect, and abuse. We emphasize raising awareness among our employees, business partners, and stakeholders and support awareness initiatives for the protection of children. We see protecting the rights of children— the most valuable trust for the future—as an indispensable part of our sustainable society approach.
  • Women’s Rights and Equal Opportunity: At Kaya Holding, we see women’s equal rights in working life, in society, and in decision-making as an important element of sustainable development. We uphold equality in recruitment, career development, training, compensation, and leadership opportunities. We value creating safe and respectful workplaces against all forms of discrimination, violence, harassment, and exclusion. With our inclusive corporate culture that supports women’s empowerment, we work for a fairer and stronger future.

Environmental Responsibility Policy

At Kaya Holding, we shape more than half a century of growth within a framework of respect for nature and the environment. With the vision of leaving future generations a livable world, we minimize the environmental impact of our operations in line with international compliance requirements.

  • Energy and Climate Management: At Kaya Holding, we see combating climate change as an important part of our corporate responsibility. We aim to reduce our environmental impact through practices that increase energy efficiency, innovative technologies, and work that optimizes resource use. We take steps to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and address energy management across all our operations with a sustainability mindset. We work to operate with a lower environmental impact and leave future generations a more livable world.
  • Carbon Neutral Room: At Kaya Holding, we see sustainable tourism as an integral part of the guest experience at our hotels. With our Carbon Neutral Room initiative, we offer guests the voluntary opportunity to offset the carbon footprint arising from their stay. Through this program we embrace environmental responsibility together with our guests, not only in our operations, and make a concrete contribution to fighting climate change. We continue to develop sustainable solutions and expand our environmentally conscious practices resolutely for a more livable future.
  • Biodiversity and Protection of Natural Life: At Kaya Holding, we see protecting biological diversity as an important part of our responsibility toward nature. In the regions where we operate, we carry out work to protect natural habitats, support the sustainability of local plant and animal species, and reduce our environmental impact. In our landscaping we prefer species suited to the region’s natural character and support the conscious use of natural resources. We see preserving nature’s richness not only as a duty today but as one of the most valuable legacies we will leave to future generations.
  • Waste Management and Circular Economy: At Kaya Holding, we see reducing waste, using resources efficiently, and strengthening recycling processes as an important part of our sustainability approach. At our hotels we implement measures to reduce waste at source and improve separation and recovery processes. With a circular economy mindset, we aim to extend the life cycle of resources, prevent waste, and minimize our environmental impact. We continue to strengthen our responsibility toward nature with more conscious practices in every area.
  • Green Hotel Practices: At Kaya Holding, we value offering a comfortable guest experience together with practices that respect nature at our hotels. We work to reduce our environmental impact in areas such as energy and water efficiency, waste management, recycling, sustainable procurement, and conscious use of resources. With our Green Hotel approach, we take responsibility not only for today’s needs but for future generations’ right to life. We continue to develop practices every day that protect natural resources, respect the environment, and support sustainable tourism.

Quality Policy

Meeting customer and stakeholder expectations at the highest level and working with an innovative, continuous improvement mindset are the foundation of our corporate quality approach. We commit to delivering world-class service in all sectors.

  • Employee Development and Investment in People: At Kaya Holding, we believe our most valuable resource is people. We build a strong organization through training that supports our employees’ professional development, career opportunities, and a culture of continuous learning. With participatory, fair, and respectful workplaces, we support our employees in realizing their potential. We aim to sustain our corporate success through our employees’ development and the value we create together.
  • Responsible Supply Chain: At Kaya Holding, we believe sustainability is not limited to our own activities alone. Therefore we evaluate our suppliers in line with quality, environment, occupational health and safety, human rights, and ethical trade principles. We value supporting local producers, increasing the use of reliable and sustainable products, and spreading responsible procurement across our entire supply chain. We continue to build long-term, trust-based relationships with business partners who share our values.
