Kaya Holding Announces TRY 50 Million Education Investment for Tomorrow’s Leaders

March 15, 2026

Kaya Holding is making another major investment in education—the cornerstone of social development and a sustainable future. It announced the creation of an additional TRY 50 million fund under the “Leaders of Tomorrow Program,” which aims to reach high-achieving young people across Turkey with limited financial means.

With a half-century legacy, Kaya Holding has made producing social benefit—not only economic value—a principle. With this new fund, it will provide non-repayable scholarships to 5,000 university students each year. The program will also offer internships abroad and mentoring opportunities to successful students.

Equal Opportunity in Education

Speaking at a press conference on the initiative, Kaya Holding Chairman of the Board Burhanettin Kaya said: “A nation’s true wealth is its well-educated, visionary youth. With our sense of corporate citizenship, we will continue to stand with our young people and advocate for equal opportunity in education.”

“We are not only building structures and facilities; we are lighting the way for the young people who will build Turkey’s bright future.”

Scholarship applications will open in September on the official website of the Kaya Education Foundation. Senior executives of Kaya Holding will also volunteer personally for the mentoring network established under the project.

Alongside its education investments, Kaya Holding remains one of Turkey’s leading brands through sustainable corporate social responsibility projects in the environment, culture, and the arts.
