Careers

At Kaya Holding, we see our employees as the most valuable part of our success; we support development, team spirit, and a sustainable career journey.

Take Your Career into the Future with Kaya Holding

A career at Kaya Holding is not just a job; it is a journey of being part of an international service culture. We offer a work environment that embraces international service standards in the sectors we operate in and prioritizes employee development.

We aim to provide our employees with development opportunities, fair and equitable treatment, a safe workplace, and a strong team culture.

We believe the employee experience is the most important force in turning guest and customer satisfaction into sustainable success, and we build a team culture that learns, develops, and grows together.
