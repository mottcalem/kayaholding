The Lifeblood of Turkish Tourism

Flawless Service, Global Vision

Tourism is the investment sector in which Kaya Holding continues its priority activities. Kaya Hotels & Resorts, through its distinctive projects, is one of the lifeblood brands not only of Kaya Holding but of Turkish and world tourism.

Kaya serves guests through a total of 14 hotels across Turkey, the TRNC, and the United Kingdom. Five of these properties operate under Kaya Palazzo Hotels & Resorts and nine under Kaya Hotels & Resorts, offering premium comfort and privileged experiences.

Known for investments shaping the future of Turkish tourism, projects by Kaya Hotels & Resorts and Kaya Palazzo Hotels & Resorts are pioneering in the sector. In 2024, Kaya Holding entered the UK and European market by acquiring the Great Nothern Hotel in King’s Cross, central London, and continues to develop tourism investments at home and abroad.
