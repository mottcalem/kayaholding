About Us

A corporate and visionary force investing in Turkey’s future, growing continuously with inspiration from its roots.

A Strong Legacy Spanning More Than Half a Century

Since its founding, Kaya Holding has operated on the principles of trust, quality, and sustainable growth. Through investments in tourism, real estate, aviation, natural resources, and services, it has become one of Turkey’s most established investment groups.

With projects delivered at home and abroad, it creates economic value while placing guest satisfaction, social benefit, and environmental responsibility at the center of how it does business.

Today, with the brands under its umbrella, Kaya Holding inspires confidence among millions of guests, thousands of employees, and business partners, and continues to grow through its strong financial structure, innovative outlook, and long-term investment strategy.
