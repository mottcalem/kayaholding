Our Project Journey of Success

1988 - 2006

The hotel construction journey that began in 1988 with Kaya Side Hotel continued with DorukKaya Hotel and mechanical facilities in 1990, Green Park Hotel in 1996, and Alanya Kaya Hotel completed the same year.

Kaya Belek opened in 1998, Kaya Select Hotel in 2000, Kaya Ramada Plaza Hotel in 2002, and Kaya Millenium Business Center was successfully completed in 2006.

2007 - 2011

In 2007, Kaya Artemis Resort—one of Northern Cyprus’s largest hotels—was delivered, and Kaya Palazzo Golf Club opened in November of the same year.

The Artemis Convention Center was completed in 2008; in 2010, Kaya İzmir Thermal & Convention Hotel and Kaya Convention Center in İzmir were finished. In 2011, Kaya Palazzo Ski & Mountain Resort—one of Turkey’s most impressive winter hotels—was built and opened.

2013 - 2014

Kaya Palazzo Golf Resort opened in Belek in 2013 as a new symbol of luxury and grandeur. In 2014, the Abant Su Gölcük facility was commissioned, bringing a natural water source to the public.

2017 - 2019

In 2017, Kaya Palazzo Resort – TRNC began welcoming guests as one of Northern Cyprus’s most exclusive holiday destinations; in 2019, Kaya Palazzo Kartalkaya Luxury Chalets opened its doors.

2020

Kaya Palazzo Belek Luxury Lagoon Suites, opened in 2020, added a new dimension to luxury accommodation with private suites opening directly onto the pool.

Within the same year, the Palazzo Lounge Kartalkaya project was also completed and opened.

2021

In 2021, the Abant Su facilities in Burdur were delivered.

2023 - Present

The Palazzo Mansions project was completed in 2023, crowning the brand’s investments in the luxury segment. As of 2024, construction of the Kaya Residence project has started and continues.

In addition, the next-generation tourism investment Kaya Palazzo Bafra, to be located in Northern Cyprus, is under construction.

Finally, the Kaya Palazzo Hotel & Convention – Ankara project is progressing at full speed, with the goal of adding value to the capital’s business and tourism life.
