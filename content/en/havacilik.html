Burhanettin Kaya Havacılık ve Ticaret A.Ş.

Burhanettin Kaya Havacılık ve Ticaret A.Ş. is one of the group companies that manages Kaya Holding’s strategic investments in aviation. It operates in Turkey’s dynamic aviation ecosystem on the principles of reliability, operational excellence, and long-term value creation.

Our company continues investment and business development activities across the sector, with a focus on commercial aviation, aircraft leasing, and aviation support services. Backed by Kaya Holding’s strong corporate structure and experience, it safely expands fleet and operational capacity through domestic and international partnerships.

Placing safety and quality standards at the forefront at every stage, Burhanettin Kaya Havacılık ve Ticaret A.Ş. invests in environmentally conscious technologies and aims for sustainable growth in aviation. With its experienced team and disciplined management approach, it remains an important part of Kaya Holding’s multi-sector vision.
