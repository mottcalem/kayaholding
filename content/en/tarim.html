Strength from the Soil

Natural and Healthy Production

Kaya Holding reflects its commitment to natural, healthy production in its business culture and has added agriculture to the sectors in which it operates.

Agriculture of the Future

Modern Greenhouse Farming and Technology

We combine traditional farming with today’s advanced technologies and operate year-round in modern greenhouses equipped with climate control and smart irrigation systems.

While protecting natural resources, we achieve high-yield ecological harvests with minimum water use and a zero-waste approach.
