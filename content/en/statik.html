Personal Data Protection

Information notice under the Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 (draft).

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Data Controller

Kaya Turistik Tesisleri Titreyengöl Otelcilik A.Ş. (“Company”) may process your personal data as data controller under the Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 (“KVKK”) within the scope described below.

Personal Data Processed

Through our website and contact channels, the following personal data may be collected:

  • Identity and contact details (name, company name, email, phone),
  • Request and message content, subject line,
  • Other information you provide via the contact form,
  • Security and usage data obtained through cookies and similar technologies (details may be provided in a separate cookie policy).

Purposes of Processing

Your personal data may be processed to receive and respond to contact requests, provide information and support, manage requests and complaints, improve service quality, fulfill legal obligations, and protect the Company’s legitimate interests.

Legal Grounds

Processing may take place where permitted under Articles 5 and 6 of the KVKK, including establishment or performance of a contract, compliance with a legal obligation, establishment or protection of a right, legitimate interests of the data controller, or your explicit consent where required.

Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to IT, hosting, email and advisory service providers, group companies, authorized public institutions, and legally authorized bodies, limited to the conditions and purposes set out in the KVKK.

Retention

Personal data is retained for the period required by the processing purpose and applicable statutory limitation periods, then deleted, destroyed, or anonymized.

Your Rights

Under Article 11 of the KVKK, you have the right to learn whether your data is processed, request information, learn the purpose of processing, know third parties to whom data is transferred, request correction of incomplete or inaccurate data, request deletion or destruction, and request compensation for damage.

Contact

To exercise your rights or ask questions, you may write to kvkk@kayaholding.com.tr. Requests will be concluded within the periods prescribed by law.
