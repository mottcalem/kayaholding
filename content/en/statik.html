Privacy Policy

Our principles regarding the processing and protection of your personal data.

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

As Kaya Holding and its affiliates, we attach the highest importance to the protection and security of the personal data of our valued guests, business partners, and website visitors. This text has been prepared to fulfill our obligation to inform you as data controller under the Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 (“KVKK”).

1. Collection and Processing of Personal Data

Your personal data may be collected by Kaya Holding through automatic or non-automatic methods, verbally, in writing, or electronically via our offices, website, social media channels, mobile applications, and similar means, in order to deliver the services we provide in the best possible way.

  • Name, surname, Turkish ID number, passport number or temporary Turkish ID number, place and date of birth, marital status, gender,
  • Address, phone number, email address, and other contact data,
  • Financial data such as payment and billing information,
  • Audio-visual data obtained from CCTV and security checkpoints.

2. Purposes of Processing Personal Data

Your collected personal data is processed for purposes including enabling you to benefit from the products and services offered by our company, carrying out necessary work by our business units, improving service quality, and fulfilling our contractual obligations.

3. Transfer of Personal Data

Your personal data may be shared within the limits set by legal regulations with organizations we cooperate with to carry out our activities, our group companies, shareholders, business partners, suppliers, legal authorities, and legally authorized public and private institutions, as required.

4. Rights of the Data Subject

Under Article 11 of the KVKK, data subjects have the right to:

  • Learn whether personal data is being processed,
  • Request information if personal data has been processed,
  • Learn the purpose of processing and whether data is used in line with that purpose,
  • Know third parties to whom personal data is transferred domestically or abroad,
  • Request correction if personal data has been processed incompletely or inaccurately.

5. Data Security

Our company takes all necessary technical and administrative security measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, use, or disclosure of personal data. Our systems are protected with up-to-date encryption and security software.

You can always contact us to submit requests or exercise your rights under the KVKK by sending an email to kvkk@kayaholding.com.tr.
