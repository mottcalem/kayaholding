Mission and Vision

Our purpose and outlook on the future on our journey of value from Turkey to the world.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

As Kaya Holding, with teams that have strong employee engagement, we aim to deliver flawless service to our customers in every sector we operate in and to create happy, meaningful experiences at every touchpoint.

By setting the standard for hospitality and corporate service quality, we move forward resolutely toward becoming a globally preferred and trusted brand—without compromising our innovative management approach.

Our Future

Our Vision

We aim to become the first choice for our stakeholders, employees, and business partners by blending our distinctive service approach—led by tourism and extending across all our sectors—with our openness to innovation, learning organization structure, and deep-rooted corporate culture.

As a value born in Turkey, we seek to carry our brand to international destinations and pursue sustainable growth worldwide with quality, reliability, and our corporate governance approach.

Our Strategic Goals

Drawing strength from our roots, these are our main focus areas on the path to 2030 and beyond.

01. Global Expansion

Crown our strong leadership in the Turkish market with international investments and increase global brand awareness.

02. Sustainability

Minimize our carbon footprint across all operations and maximize the use of green energy.

03. Digital Transformation

Integrate the latest technologies into our business processes to raise efficiency and customer experience to the highest level.

04. People-Centered Approach

Keep employee satisfaction and continuous development at the heart of our culture to attract the industry’s best talent.
